When Did Hut 8 Most Recently Announce Their BTC Holdings?

On May 6, 2024, Hut 8 (Nasdaq: HUT) (TSX: HUT) announced that they had selfed mined 148 bitcoins during April of 2024.(2).

Hut 8 also announced it would be ending it's managed services at the Kearney, Nebraska and Granbury, Texas mining sites on April 30, 2024.

While Hut 8 has a goal of holding its bitcoins long term, rather than selling them for cash, they are prepared to sell if necessary for debt management and mining hardware improvements.
