What Does Fidelity Do?

Fidelity Investments is an mutual fund company that provides services to individual and corporate investors through their investment products. They have offices in North America, Europe, Asia and Australia.

Who Owns Fidelity?

Fidelity is 49% owned by the Johnson Family and the other 51% is employee owned. Abigail Johnson has been the CEO of Fidelity Investments since October of 2014.

Does Fidelity Mine Bitcoin?

Fidelity is not involved in the mining of Bitcoin.

Does Fidelity have Ethereum investment products?

Fidelity has applied for a Ethereum ETF Fund and is awaiting SEC approval. Fidelity also allows customers to buy and sell bitcoin and ethereum through a Fidelity Crypto account.
