How Much Bitcoin Does Tesla Own?

Tesla owns 10,725 bitcoins as of September 2023.

Did Tesla Sell Its Bitcoin?

Tesla held 42,902 bitcoins, but in July 2022 it was announced that they sold 75% of their holdings bringing the company's total down to 10,725. The Bitcoin price fell about 1.7% on the news(1).

When Did Tesla Buy Bitcoin?

Tesla first bought Bitcoin in February 2021(2).

Tesla also said it will start accepting payments in Bitcoin in exchange for its products “subject to applicable laws and initially on a limited basis.” That would make Tesla the first major automaker to do so. The $1.5 billion worth of bitcoin will give Tesla liquidity in the cryptocurrency once it starts accepting it for payments.

Does Tesla Accept Bitcoin?

Tesla did accept Bitcoin for its cars at one point. But, citing environmental concerns, the company removed the option to pay through Bitcoin. They do have plans to re-launch Bitcoin payments soon(4).

How Much Has Tesla Made on Bitcoin?

It's not clear at which prices Tesla bought its bitcoins. Therefore, we don't know if they have made money on their investment.

How Many Teslas Have Been Bought with Bitcoin?

Tesla has not reported if they sold any cars for bitcoins.
